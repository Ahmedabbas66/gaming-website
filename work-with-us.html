Frequently Asked
Questions

I don't live in any of the cities with a Riot
office. Is there an opportunity to work remotely?
It's highly unlikely that we'll be able to consider you for a role that isn't

based out of one of the locations where we currently do business. If

you're open to relocating, please make sure to indicate that in your

application.​

I want to work at one of Riot's global offices
but I’m not a citizen of that country. Is that
possible?
Working globally depends on the immigration restrictions of the relevant

country/region. If you apply to a role in a global office, those recruiting

teams will respond if they see a match.

Can I apply for multiple jobs?
We want applicants to think deeply about their skills and interest—and

how they line up with the jobs available at Riot. It's not a good sign if you

find yourself applying to as many open positions as possible.
